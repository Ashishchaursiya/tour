  • Duration: 7 Days / Nights .
  • Price: 35000 /- per person
     

Day 1
Arrival
The first day will be partly at travel and partly at leisure.

Your Kuala Lumpur package holidays commence with your arrival at Kuala Lumpur airport. From there, proceed to your next flight to Langkawi which will be your final destination of the day. From the Langkawi airport, you’ll be transferred to the hotel. Have some rest and stay overnight at the hotel.


Day 2
Enjoy the amazing Langkawi Island Tour

Witness the beautiful Langkawi Island and enjoy a great soothing time.
Begin your Kuala Lumpur Langkawi tour package with a visit to the famous Langkawi island. A delightful breakfast awaits you in the morning. After breakfast, you’ll further proceed to the Langkawi Island Tour. Enjoy a number of water sport activities along with a peaceful time in the clutches of the sea.

Day 3
A visit to the Sky Bridge

Enjoy the renowned cable ride to the sky bridge which displays a picturesque landscape.
Have breakfast in the morning from where your Kuala Lumpur tour package will further proceed to the famous Sky Bridge. Enjoy the cable car ride which will take you to the sky bridge, offering a view like never seen before. In a single view, one can see an abundance of lush green forest, a large view of the sea as well as a majestic view of the clouds. Overnight stay at Langkawi.

Day 4
Langkawi to Genting

Proceed to your next destination, Genting.
Have breakfast in the morning. You’ll be transferred to the airport to board a flight back to Kuala Lumpur. Transfer to Genting from Kuala Lumpur and enroute enjoy the well known Cable Car designed by the Weather Permit which offers magnificent views of nature’s beautiful bounties. Overnight at Genting.

Day 5
Genting to Kuala Lumpur

Today proceed to the heart of Malaysia, Kuala Lumpur.
Your trip of Kuala Lumpur, today, will take you finally to the city. Have breakfast in the morning and transfer to Kuala Lumpur from Genting. Enjoy a leisure overnight stay at Kuala Lumpur.

Day 6
Explore Kuala Lumpur

Explore the charms of Kuala Lumpur with a detailed city tour.
Have breakfast in the morning and proceed to an amazingly detailed and elaborative city tour of Kuala Lumpur displaying almost all the local attractions of the city, the most famous one being the KL Tower. After an exhausting day, have a sound sleep at the hotel where you’ll be staying overnight.

Day 7
Departure

It’s the day of departure.
This amazing Kuala Lumpur tour package ends on this day. Enjoy breakfast in the morning and after that, you’ll be transferred to the airport to board the departure flight to your respective destinations.
