Kerala (6 Days / 5 Nights)

6 D / 5 N | Location : India
  • Overview
  • Itinerary
  • Notes
  • Travel around the attention-grabbing highlights of Kochi.
  • Explore the scenic beauty & attractions of Munnar.
  • Pass through the fragrant Spice Gardens of Thekkady.
  • Spend time bathing the baby elephants at the Elephant Camp.
  • Explore the scenic beauty & Backwaters of Kumarakom.

Day 1
Munnar: Arrival and quality leisure time

Impart a beautiful beginning to the new phase of your life with this Munnar, Thekkady, Alleppey package. 
On your arrival at the Kochi Airport/ Ernakulam Railway Station, enjoy being greeted by an agent’s representative. From here, you will be taken to Munnar to begin your trip amidst the hillscapes. The scenery glams up your journey from Kochi to Munnar. The aroma of spice plantations and symphony of Valara and Cheeyappara waterfalls will add a magical touch to your first trip of togetherness. 

Once you reach Munnar, the pleasant weather, tea plantations, streams, and waterfalls will extend you a hearty welcome. Now, complete the check-in formalities at the hotel, and relax. Rest of the day is at leisure as per this Munnar, Thekkady, Alleppey  package. Delight in this time as your heart desires. You can explore the surroundings holding hands and enjoy the serene solitude. When the sun sets, come back to the hotel for a restful night.  
Distance from Kochi to Munnar: 130 km (approx.)
Travel time: 4 hours (approx.)


Day 2
Munnar: Visit the tourists hotspots

The nature’s glamor awaits you as planned by this Kochi, Munnar, Thekkady, Alleppey package for Family.
Enjoy a scrumptious breakfast together and be prepared to discover the masterpieces with which nature has blessed this quaint hill town. The sightseeing spree takes you to the most sought after attractions of Munnar such as Mattupetty Dam and Lake. The tea garden around mirrors in the crystal clear still water of this dam, enhancing the charm of your visit. Photo Point is another attraction where woods, thick tea plantations, rolling hills, and gurgling rivulets create an ideal environment for love birds, where they have numerous ‘clickable’ moments. You will also be taken to Nursery Garden, Echo Point, and Eravikulam National Park. A view of the beautiful Anaimudi Peak adds to your day. 

In the evening, enjoy a leisurely stroll on the spice/tea trail according to your Munnar, Alleppey package with visits to Thekkady and Kochi. This is the best spot to delight in the beautiful sunset with the love of your life. Once you are done with sightseeing, return to the hotel for a refreshing overnight stay. 
Note: Tea Museum remains closed on Mondays. 

Day 3
Thekkady: Arrival and a memorable day

This Alleppey, Kochi, Munnar and Thekkady package  takes you for an unprecedented experience. 
Wake up to a wholesome breakfast and check-out from the hotel. Today, you will be transferred to Thekkady, an astounding location of Periyar National Park near Idukki district. Delighting in the scenery presented by spice plantations, waterfalls, grasslands, and misty mountains, you will reach Thekkady. Now, check-in to the hotel and relax for some time before you will be taken to Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. The wilderness and a rich variety of flora and fauna will manifold your delight. Towards the evening, set off to Kumily village to visit its spice market and also buy some. For a dose of adrenaline, go trekking in Ramakkalmedu, which is among the most beautiful hill stations of Kerala. After a pleasurable day with your sweetheart, return to the hotel and head to your room for a peaceful sleep. 

Optional: Bamboo rafting at Periyar Lake or trekking in Ramakkalmedu (charges extra)
Distance from Munnar to Thekkady: 94 km (approx.)
Travel Time: 3 hours (approx.)
Distance from Thekkady to Ramakkalmedu: 38.8 km (approx.)
Travel Time: 1 hour (approx.)

Day 4
Alleppey: Enjoy a houseboat experience

An offbeat experience invites you on this day of your Munnar and Alleppey package.
Savor a healthy breakfast before checking out from the hotel. Now, you will be taken to Alleppey, which is among the dreamiest destinations of Kerala. The charismatic surroundings will leave you awestruck. Now, check-in to the houseboat to impart a unique flavor to your  Refresh and relish a lavish lunch onboard. Your post lunch time will treat you to a splendid ride as you cruise along  Kerala backwaters with your beloved. 
Later, a romantic dinner will enhance the flavor of your day on the houseboat. After adding flavors to your bond, this day will also add some  restful moments to your visit as you slip into your comfy bed for the night. Indeed, an unforgettable Alleppey houseboat stay.

Distance from Munnar to Alleppey: 175.5 km (approx.)
Travel Time: 4 hours, 30 minutes (approx.).

Day 5
Kochi: A short and exciting trip

The next leg of your Alleppey, Kochi, Thekkady, Munnar itinerary for holiday takes you to Kochi. 
Arise to a lovely breakfast and disembark the houseboat as you will be transferred to Kochi today. Upon arrival in Kochi, check-in to your hotel and unwind for sometime. Now this Kochi tour itinerary takes you for a sightseeing spree. You will be visiting attractions such as St. Francis Church, Mattancherry Palace, the historical town of Fort Cochin, and Marine Drive. You can enjoy a leisurely stroll with your soulmate hand in hand on Marine Drive. 
After a happening day, come back to the hotel for a restful night. 

Distance from Alleppey to Kochi: 53 km (approx.) 
Travel Time: 1 hour, 15 minutes (approx.)  

Day 6
Kochi: Departure back home

Your lovely trip concludes in Kochi today
After breakfast, check-out from the hotel. Get transferred to airport/railway station to board your flight/train for a journey back home.
