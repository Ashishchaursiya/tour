Darjeeling / Gangtok

6 D / 5 N | Location : India
  • Overview
  • Itinerary
  • Notes

Gangtok is a popular hill destination in the north eastern region of India where you can view the natural beauty dotted with lakes , mountains and specially catch a glimpse of the majestic Kanchenjunga which is the third highest peak in the world.. 

  • Darjeeling- 2N
  • Gangtok -3N
  • Price: Rs.25000/per person
  • HOTEL YUMA : Amenities: Wi-Fi, TV, , Breakfast
  • SIKKIM RETREAT GANGTOK : Amenities: Wi-Fi, TV, , Breakfast

Pick up and drop from Bagdogra Airport/NJP Rly station.

  1. Nights stay at Darjeeling hotel.
  2. Nights stay at Gangtok hotel.
  3. Breakfast & Dinner.
  4. Sightseeing as per itinerary.
  5. All toll and driver charges included

Day  1: NJP Railway Station / Bagdogra (IXB) Airport – Darjeeling (About 90 kms / 03 Hrs. drives)

On arrival at NJP Railway station / IXB airport welcome to our representative & transfer to Darjeeling. Check-in to your hotel & rest of the day free at leisure. Night halt at Darjeeling.

Day 2: Darjeeling (Local sightseeing)

Early Morning at 4.00 am visit Tiger hill (sunrise point over Mt. Khanchendzongna), on the way back visit Ghoom Monastery & Batasia Loop. After breakfast half day city tour covering Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, P.N. Zoological Park (closed on Thursday) Tenzing Rock,Tea Garden (outer view), Pace Pagoda, Japanese Temple & Rope Way (Ticket Guest Own). Afternoon back to hotel & enjoy shopping in famous mall road by walk. Night halt at Darjeeling.

Day 3: Darjeeling – Gangtok (About 115kms/ 04 Hrs. drive) & Gangtok sightseeing

After breakfast drive to Gangtok. Check-in to your hotel. Afternoon visit Bonjhakri water Falls, Flower show, Handicraft Centre, Dro-Dul Chorten And Institute of Tibetology (Sunday Closed). Evening back to hotel & Rest of the day free at leisure. Night halt at Gangtok.

Day 4: Gangtok (Trip to Tsomgo Lake + Baba Mandir-New) (optional tour to Nathula Pass-on direct payment basis)

This Morning start for a half day trip to Tsomgo lake (12400ft), which is only 32kms from Gangtok City, the lake is almost about 1Km long & 15 mtrs deep. The lake is considered sacred by the local people; further 12Kms visit the Baba Harvajan Singh Mandir (New). Afternoon back to the hotel. Night halt at Gangtok.

Day 5:Gangtok

Today your day at leisure you can explore your day at your own pace.


Day 6: Gangtok - Bagdogra (IXB) Airport (125 kms / 04 hrs drive)

After breakfast check out from hotel and transfer to NJP railway station / IXB airport for your onward journey.
