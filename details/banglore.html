BANGALORE - MYSORE - OOTY

Bangalore( Arrive )- Bangalore(01 Night)- Mysore(02 Nights)- Ooty(03 Nights)- Bangalore / Coimbatore Drop

  • Price: Rs.35000
  • Duration:7Days/6 Nights

Day 01

Iskon temple Arrive Bangalore -

Bangalore Local Sightseeing Pickup from arrival. Transfer to a hotel, after fresh'n up proceed to visit Lalbagh, Bull Temple, Vidansaudha(drive past), Vishveshwarya Technological Museum, Art Gallery, Cubbon Park, Indira Gandhi Musical Fountain Park and ISKON temple. Overnight stay at Bangalore.

Day 02 :Dariya-Daulat-Bagh

Bangalore to Mysore Sightseeing After breakfast proceed to Mysore. En route visit Srirangapatna(Dariya Daulat Bagh, Gumbaz, Tippu Fort and Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple). On arrival check in to hotel. Visit Maharaja's Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill (Goddess Chamundeshwari), Mysore Zoo, Big Bull, Saint Philomena's Church (majestic churches in India) and Jagan Mohan Palace (Art Gallery). Evening proceed to Brindavan Garden (botanical park with musical fountain). Overnight stay at Mysore. Distance between Bangalore to Mysore is 160 Kms (3.5 hours) by road.

Day 03:Maharajas-Mysore-Palace

Mysore Local Sightseeing After breakfast visit Maharaja's Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill (Goddess Chamundeshwari), Mysore Zoo, Big Bull, Saint Philomena's Church (majestic churches in India) and Jagan Mohan Palace (Art Gallery). Overnight stay at Mysore.

Day 04:Rose-Garden

Mysore to Ooty

After morning breakfast checkout and proceed to Ooty, En-route visit Bandipur National Forest & Madumali Forest. On arrival check in to hotel. Visit Rose Garden, Ooty Lake and Botanical Garden. Overnight stay at Ooty.

Distance between Mysore to Ooty is 150 Kms / 4 Hours by road

Day 05: Sims-park

Ooty & Coonoor Local Sightseeing

After breakfast Proceed to Coonoor. Coonoor Sight seeing(Sim's Park, Lam's Rock, Dolphin Nose and Ketti Valley - beautiful scenic views of the valley). Also visit Tea factory, Tea Museum and Doddabetta Peak - highest peak of the Nilgiri mountain range. Overnight stay at Ooty.

Distance between Ooty to Coonoor is 50 Kms / 2 Hours by road

Day 06:Paykara-water-falls

Ooty - Paykara visit

After breakfast proceed to Paykara water falls, Paykara dam and filmy chakkar sight seeing. Overnight stay at Ooty.

Day 07: Airport-drop-Bangalore

Ooty to Bangalore Drop / Coimbatore Drop After breakfast checkout and proceed to Coimbatore / Bangalore to board your train or flight.
