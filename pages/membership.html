Holidays Xpress Club Membership

We at vintage vibes understand that when you are ready to book the exciting vacation that you have been looking forward to, you would like to work with people who can handle details, save you money and ensure that your travel needs are met. So you can be assured of being in right hands at vintage Vibes.

Membership Card

You will find a card inside your welcome kit which is the Vintage Vibes Membership Card. It helps us to identify you easily. Do carry your membership card everytime you travel with vintage Vibes.

Plan Your Holiday

At vintage Vibes, we like to keep things exciting and new. We keep adding more and more places to our offerings. Make sure when you plan your holiday at the hotel of your choice, inform us in advance. Plan your international holiday atleast 6 months in advance so that everything goes smoothly. Inform us about everything in advance, like the number of people, place, hotel selections, activities to do and any other requirement.


Booking Your Holiday

Remember to present your membership card to us. Carry along all the required documents like membership card, photo identity proof, applicable visas and passport. We have variety of options for destinations, hotels, dates so that you can choose from your convenience.
